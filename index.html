Services

  Demo VR Technologies

VR technology is objectively cool, but it's pretty expensive to get your hands it. At NightView, you can demo VR technologies and games in store for free for 15 minutes at a time between 10pm and 3pm. Try new tech and new games. Every week we'll have a new VR game for you to try, and we'll showcase new tech if we can get our hands on it.

  Negotiable Prices

Gaming is a very expensive industry to be in, but at NightView Video Games we allow you to bargain a price if you think that it should be cheaper. If you can show proof of a non-third party seller selling a game for cheaper, we will sell it to you for that price. However, it must be shown in-store and screenshots will not be considered proof. We provide our customers with a place to buy video games at a reasonable price.

  Trivia Night

With every big realease, we like to celebrate with the community. The friday the week before a big realease, we will host trivia night. Either related to previous entries of the series, general game knowledge or lore, or real life events in the gaming community. Trivia night will be either 1 on 1's or in groups staring at 5pm. Prizes will awarded!

  Membership Perks

Sign up for a membership and recieve rewards point. You can also receive perks for bringing in and exchanging used games. You'll be payed 75% of the amount you originally payed only if you have a receipt, if not you'll be payed back 75% of the stores price. You will also be given different kinds of perks towards your membership. Perks include longer VR Demo time, more discounts, raffle prizes, etc.
